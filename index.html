About me

Born in Atlanta, Georgia. Moved to Colorado in the 1st grade. Lived there for a couple years, just long enough to learn that I loved snowboarding. Moved back to Atlanta and have lived here ever since.Though I do not enjoy the traffic I couldn't imagine living anywhere else.
My work career started at the age of 16, working 9-5 everyday in a weilding shop, I realized i loved getting my hands dirty. Since then I have not looked back.
